Class Measurement

Measurement tournaments pit the player being measured against others in order to assess that player's performance at a game. They are used to evaluate how well the players play by confronting them with the opponents, rotating their roles in the matches.

A measurement tournament makes every player play matchCount matches for each role in the game against all possible combinations of opponents.
